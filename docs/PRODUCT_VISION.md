# Product Vision

## Mission
Build a scalable, real-time AI-powered trading intelligence platform focused on signal analysis, risk filtering, and real-time market insights.

## Target Users
- **Intraday Traders**: Demanding real-time signals and micro-trend analysis.
- **Swing Traders**: Requiring multi-candle aggregation and high-conviction signals.
- **Retail Traders**: Needing clean alerts and risk-aware trade plans.
- **Quantitative Enthusiasts**: Looking for customizable indicators and backtesting frameworks.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Real-Time Signal Generation**: Ingestion to signal execution path completed in milliseconds.
- **AI-Assisted Analysis**: Dynamic market insights and structural breakdowns powered by LLMs.
- **Fast Alert Delivery**: Seamless routing to Telegram and Discord.
- **Risk-Aware Trading Insights**: Strong validation boundary ensuring all signals strictly conform to risk constraints.
- **Scalable Analytics Platform**: A modular design positioned for effortless transition to a distributed microservices network.
